An Introduction to Kpop

What is Kpop?

  • Kpop is an abbreviation for Korean pop or Korean popular music
  • It can consist of elements from musical genres such as:
    • Electronica
    • Hip Hop
    • Pop
    • R&B
    • Rock

In Kpop, there aren't just solo musicians and bands but there are also Kpop groups.

The first Kpop group, Seo Taiji and Boys, was formed in 1992 and from then on Kpop groups became part of Korea's music culture.
